Prep Time: 15 mins

Cook Time: 35 mins

Servings: 6

Steps to Make a Cake

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Grease and flour the cake pan.
  3. Mix flour, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.
  4. Cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  5. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each.
  6. Mix in vanilla extract.
  7. Gradually add the dry ingredients and milk, alternating.
  8. Pour batter into the prepared pan.
  9. Bake for 30–35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  10. Cool the cake before frosting or serving.

Ingredients for Cake

  • 1 and 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter (softened)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
